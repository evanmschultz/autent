# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors and maintainers should keep this project technically rigorous, respectful, and easy to work in.

Expected behavior includes:

- discussing ideas directly and respectfully
- assuming good intent while still being precise
- welcoming thoughtful questions and corrections
- focusing criticism on code, design, or process rather than people

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or intimidation
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- personal attacks
- deliberate disruption of project collaboration

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other community interactions around `autent`.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this standard.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email the maintainer privately at `evanmschultz@gmail.com`.
